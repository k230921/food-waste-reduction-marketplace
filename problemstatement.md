#  Food Waste Reduction Marketplace

##  Problem Title
**Food Waste and Inefficient Redistribution of Surplus Food in Australia**

##  Problem Description
A large amount of usable food is wasted daily by restaurants, grocery stores, and cafés.

##  Target Users
- Small-to-medium food businesses  
- Students and low-income individuals  
- Environmentally conscious consumers  

##  Current Solutions
- End-of-day discounts  
- Food donations  
- Third-party apps  

##  Why Current Solutions Fail
- No real-time visibility  
- Inefficient processes  
- Low awareness  

##  Proposed Solution
A web-based platform where businesses can list surplus food in real time.
